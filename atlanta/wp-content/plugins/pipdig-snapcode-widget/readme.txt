Add a Snapchat Snapcode to your site. Get more followers!

== Description ==

The easy way to add your **Snapchat Snapcode** to any widget area on your site.  Simply download your [Snapcode](https://accounts.snapchat.com/accounts/snapcodes?type=png) and upload it to the widget, and you're ready to go!

**What's a Snapchat Snapcode?**

Snapchat introduced Snapcodes as an easy way to add friends on Snapchat. Simply point your Snapchat camera at a Snapcode and tap your screen to add!

This plugin will ensure that your Snapcode is displayed at the optimum size so that scanning with a phone will work correctly.

This free Snapchat Widget for WordPress was created by [pipdig](https://www.pipdig.co/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=wprepo&utm_campaign=snapchat). We are in no way affiliated with Snapchat. You're welcome to tweet us [@pipdig](https://twitter.com/pipdig) if you have any questions about the widget, or post on the [support forum](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/pipdig-snapcode-widget) if you need any help :)

== Installation ==

1. Go to 'Plugins > Add New' in your WordPress dashobard. Search for "Snapchat Snapcode Widget" and install this plugin.
2. Go to 'Appearance > Widgets' in the dashboard and add the widget called "Snapchat Snapcode".
3. Download the PNG image of your Snapcode from [this link](https://accounts.snapchat.com/accounts/snapcodes?type=png).
4. Upload the PNG image to the widget options in your WordPress dashboard.
