THE SPA

With two locations in prestigious area of Buckhead and north of the city in Roswell, we are an intimate skincare and eyelash center, providing the most sought after lash extensions today; courtesy of a Master Licensed Esthetician and Advanced Certified Lash Stylist who’s ranked among the top in the eyelash industry by Xtreme Lashes™. Additional services include Permanent Makeup, results driven Skin and Body Care Treatments, and more. Catering to the ultimate client who values luxury beauty and spa services, Atlanta Eye Candy is the one of a kind. This is THE place to close your eyes and awaken relaxed, refreshed and looking your best!

Atlanta Eye Candy boasts a contemporary approach to the beauty and wellness industry. As an intimate eyelash, skincare and medspa center, we provide the most sought after specialty services. Our Team of beauty and skincare professionals include Medical Estheticians, Master Estheticians, Certified Laser Technicians, Master Lash Stylist and Licensed Makeup Artists. Your customized look is our pleasure as we consult with you to achieve the look you desire!

Please contact us by email and we will be happy to discuss your beauty and esthetic needs or call to schedule your appointment today at 877-379-3365!
